The Jurney of Life

God created the cow, and he said to the cow, "I have created you as a cow. You need to go to the field with the farmer and work under the sun all day. I will give you a lifespan of 60 years." The cow disagreed, "What? Such a hard life! You want me to endure for 60 years? I only need 20 years, and you can take back the other 40 years." God agreed.

The next hour, God created the dog. God said to the dog, "You have to sit at the door of your house all day, and if someone comes in, bark loudly. I will give you a lifespan of 20 years." The dog was not happy and said, "What? Sitting at the door all day? Definitely not! I only need 10 years, and the other 10 years you can keep." God agreed.

The next hour, God created the monkey. He said to the monkey, "As a monkey, you must entertain the guests, play monkey tricks, and make people happy. I will give you a lifespan of 25 years." The monkey wouldn't do it and said, "What? Make people laugh, make faces, and perform tricks? 10 years is enough, and the remaining 15 years you keep. " God agreed.

The next hour, God created human beings. God said to the human beings, "You just need to eat, drink, have fun, rest, and sleep, and live comfortably. You don't have to do anything else, just enjoy to the fullest. I will give you a lifespan of 20 years." Human beings objected and said, "What? Such a good life, eating, drinking, having fun, resting, and sleeping comfortably. Only 20 years? No." God said, "How about this? The cow gives you back 40 years, the dog gives you back 10 years, and the monkey also gives you back 15 years." "Just give me all their returned time so that I can live for 85 years," said the human being. God agreed.

Now, do you understand? The first 20 years are the days of being human, enjoying life comfortably. Then, the next 40 years are the days of being a cow, working hard all day long to support the family. The next 15 years are the days of being a monkey, making faces to entertain grandchildren until they grow up and leave you. The last 10 years are the days of being a dog, staying at home all day, sitting at the door, and nagging at others.
